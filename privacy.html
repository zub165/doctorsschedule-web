1. Introduction

Welcome to the Doctor Schedule App ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Information: Name, phone number, specialty (for doctors)
  • Medical Information: Doctor schedules, patient appointments, medical records (if applicable)
  • Location Data: Hospital/clinic locations, preferred work locations

2.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our app:

  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage patterns and preferences
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app features used)
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our scheduling services
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account
  • Communication: To send you notifications about appointments and schedule changes
  • Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app
  • Support: To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly consent to sharing
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in app operation
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and authorization systems
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and employee training

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where retention is required by law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: View your personal information in the app settings
  • Correction: Edit your profile information directly in the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account or specific data through the app or by contacting us
  • Portability: Export your data using the "Export My Data" feature in settings
  • Partial Deletion: Delete specific data types (schedules, appointments, locations) without deleting your entire account
  • Opt-out: Control notification preferences in the app settings

How to Exercise Your Rights: Most of these rights can be exercised directly through the app by going to Settings -> Data Management. For additional assistance, contact us at zub165@yahoo.com.

8. Data Deletion and Account Removal

How to Request Data Deletion:

Complete Account Deletion:

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data:

  1. Open the Doctor Schedule app
  2. Go to Settings -> Data Management
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation prompts
  5. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted

Alternative Method: Contact us directly at zub165@yahoo.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Partial Data Deletion:

To request deletion of specific data without deleting your entire account:

  1. Open the Doctor Schedule app
  2. Go to Settings -> Data Management
  3. Select "Manage My Data"
  4. Choose the specific data you want to delete:
    • Old Schedules (schedules older than 1 year)
    • Appointment History (completed appointments)
    • Location Preferences (saved location preferences)
  5. Confirm your selection

Alternative Method: Contact us at zub165@yahoo.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"

Data Export:

To download a copy of your data:

  1. Open the Doctor Schedule app
  2. Go to Settings -> Data Management
  3. Select "Export My Data"
  4. Your data will be prepared and displayed for download

Important Information:

  • Data Types Deleted: All personal information, medical records, schedules, and appointment data associated with your account
  • Data Types Retained: Anonymous usage statistics and data required for legal compliance
  • Retention Period: Data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request
  • Verification Required: We may require identity verification before processing deletion requests
  • Processing Time: Account deletion is immediate, partial deletion within 24 hours

For complete data deletion instructions, please visit our dedicated Data Deletion page.
